DORAEMON

It is a Japanese manga and anime series about a robotic cat from the future named Doraemon, who travels back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi. Equipped with futuristic gadgets, Doraemon assists Nobita in navigating daily challenges and improving his life. The series combines humor, adventure, and moral lessons in a heartwarming way.
