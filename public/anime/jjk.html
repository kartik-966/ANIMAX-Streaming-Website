JUJUTSU KAISEN

It follows high school student Yuji Itadori, who joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to combat Curses, malevolent spirits born from human negativity. After ingesting a powerful Curse, he gains extraordinary abilities and becomes entwined in the battle against evil. The series blends action, supernatural elements, and dark themes.
