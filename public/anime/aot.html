ATTACK ON TITAN

It is a Japanese manga series set in a dystopian world where humanity resides within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends who join the military to fight these Titans after their hometown is destroyed. As the series progresses, they uncover dark secrets about the Titans and the true nature of their world. The narrative explores themes of survival, freedom, and the human condition.
